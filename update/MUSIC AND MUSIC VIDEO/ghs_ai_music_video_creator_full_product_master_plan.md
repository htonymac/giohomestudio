# 1. Product Working Title

## GHS AI Music Video Creator

---

# 2. Product Summary

GHS AI Music Video Creator is a dedicated music-video creation system inside GioHomeStudio that expands the existing music generation capability into a full AI-assisted music video workflow.

The goal is to allow a user to:
- create a song inside GHS or import an existing song
- generate a music video concept around that song
- choose visual style, story direction, performance style, and scene structure
- use AI image, AI video, motion scenes, lyrics, narration, captions, and commercial-style visual sequences
- review drafts before heavy rendering or credit use
- export music videos for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, WhatsApp status, Telegram, and promotional use

This should not feel like a random video generator.
It should feel like a **music-to-visual storytelling studio**.

# 8. Main User Journey

## Full Flow
1. user creates music in GHS or imports a track
2. GHS analyzes the music
3. GHS identifies mood, tempo, length, structure, and possible visual direction
4. GHS suggests music video options
5. user selects a video mode
6. GHS creates a storyboard draft
7. GHS shows:
   - video concept
   - scene structure
   - art style
   - visual references
   - lyric/caption structure
   - narration or intro option
   - estimated credits
8. user edits or approves the plan
9. GHS generates preview clips or draft frames
10. user reviews
11. user approves final rendering
12. credits are deducted after approval
13. final video renders
14. user exports, schedules, or publishes

# 9. Expanded Creation Modes

GHS should support these practical user-facing creation modes inside Studio:

## 9.1 Text to Music
User types an idea, theme, genre, mood, or lyric seed. GHS creates music.

## 9.2 Lyrics to Music
User pastes lyrics and GHS builds the musical structure around them.

## 9.3 Audio to Music Video
User uploads or selects audio, and GHS turns it into a music video workflow.

## 9.4 Text and Audio to Video
User provides a written concept plus an audio track. GHS uses both to build a stronger storyboard and output video.

## 9.5 Audio to Text and Video
GHS analyzes uploaded audio, generates structured text understanding from it, then uses that understanding to drive scene creation, lyric/caption timing, and final video generation.

## 9.6 Image and Music Video
User selects one or more images and a music track. GHS turns them into a visual music sequence with motion, timing, transitions, captions, and optional narration.

## 9.7 Image, Voice, and Music Video
User provides:
- image(s)
- narration voice or voice track
- music track

GHS builds a mixed storytelling video using visuals, voice, and music together.

## 9.8 Image and Animation with Music
User starts from still images. GHS animates them and synchronizes the motion to the music.

## 9.9 Children Music Video Mode
A dedicated mode for simple, bright, safe, learning-driven children content.

Examples:
- ABC song videos
- counting song videos
- phonics music videos
- colors and shapes songs
- simple moral songs
- animal learning songs
- nursery rhyme style videos

This mode should use:
- bright visuals
- safe pacing
- clean lyrics
- simple animation
- strong subtitle clarity
- child-safe prompts and filters

## 9.10 Commercial Music Video Mode
Music-backed promo content for products, services, events, fashion, and property.

# 15. Video Generation Methods

## Method A — Pure AI Video Generation
Generate scenes directly from prompts and storyboard.

## Method B — AI Image to Video
Generate key images first, then animate them into music-aligned scenes.

## Method C — Uploaded Media + AI Enhancement
Use user’s real clips/images and enhance, cut, stylize, subtitle, and sync them to music.

## Method D — Hybrid Build
Mix:
- AI video scenes
- AI images
- real user footage
- motion graphics
- lyric overlays
- subtitles
- transitions

Hybrid should become the strongest and most flexible long-term mode.
