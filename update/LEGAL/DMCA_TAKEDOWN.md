## 1. Introduction

GioHomeStudio respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. This policy establishes the procedure for reporting copyright infringement on the GioHomeStudio platform, consistent with the requirements of the United States Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), 17 U.S.C. § 512, as well as the Nigerian Copyright Act 2022 (as amended).

While GioHomeStudio operates under Nigerian law, it adopts DMCA-compliant procedures as industry best practice for handling copyright complaints globally.

## 3. How to File a Copyright Takedown Notice

To report content on the GioHomeStudio platform that you believe infringes your copyright, you must submit a written notice to the Designated Copyright Agent containing all of the following information:

### 3.1 Required Fields (DMCA § 512(c)(3) and Nigerian Copyright Act 2022)

1. **Your identity:** Your full legal name and contact information, including email address and postal address.

2. **Identification of the copyrighted work:** A description of the copyrighted work that you believe has been infringed, including any copyright registration number if available.

3. **Identification of the infringing content:** A description of the content that you believe is infringing, with sufficient specificity to allow us to locate it on the platform. Where possible, provide a direct URL or content identifier.

4. **Good faith statement:** A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the content described is not authorised by the copyright owner, the copyright owner's agent, or applicable law.

5. **Accuracy and authority statement:** A statement, made under penalty of perjury (or under the penalties prescribed by Nigerian law for false statutory declarations), that:
   - The information in the notice is accurate, and
   - You are the copyright owner or are authorised to act on behalf of the copyright owner

6. **Signature:** Your physical or electronic signature (or the signature of a person authorised to act on behalf of the copyright owner)

**Notices that do not contain all required fields will not be processed.**

## 4. Timeline and Process

### 4.1 Acknowledgement

We will acknowledge receipt of a complete and valid takedown notice within **24 hours** of receipt during business days. Receipt does not constitute a determination that the notice is valid.

### 4.2 Investigation

We will investigate all complete notices within **7 business days** of acknowledgement. During investigation we may:

- Review the content against the claimed copyright
- Contact the user who uploaded the content
- Request additional information from the complainant

### 4.3 Action Pending Dispute

Where the content is facially infringing and the notice is complete, GioHomeStudio may remove or disable access to the content pending resolution of the dispute. Removal during investigation does not constitute an admission of infringement.

### 4.4 Resolution

Following investigation, GioHomeStudio will:

- Remove the content permanently if infringement is confirmed
- Restore the content if a valid counter-notice is received (see Section 5)
- Notify both parties of the outcome

## 5. Counter-Notice Procedure

If you believe that content you uploaded was removed or disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification, you may submit a written counter-notice to the Designated Copyright Agent containing the following:

1. **Your identity:** Your full legal name, postal address, and email address.

2. **Identification of the removed content:** A description of the content that was removed and its location on the platform before removal.

3. **Good faith statement:** A statement, made under penalty of perjury (or under applicable Nigerian law), that you have a good faith belief that the content was removed or disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification.

4. **Jurisdiction statement:** A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the Federal High Court of Nigeria (or, for users outside Nigeria, a court with jurisdiction over your location) for any action by the complainant.

5. **Signature:** Your physical or electronic signature.

**Upon receipt of a valid counter-notice**, GioHomeStudio will:

- Forward the counter-notice to the original complainant
- Restore the removed content within 10–14 business days unless the complainant notifies us that they have filed a court action to restrain the activity

## 6. Repeat Infringer Policy — Three Strikes

GioHomeStudio maintains a repeat infringer policy in accordance with DMCA § 512(i) and the Nigerian Copyright Act 2022.

| Strike | Action |
|---|---|
| First substantiated infringement notice | Written warning; infringing content removed |
| Second substantiated infringement notice | Temporary account suspension (14 days); all infringing content removed |
| Third substantiated infringement notice | Permanent account termination; all content archived for legal purposes |

"Substantiated" means a notice that was not successfully countered by a valid counter-notice and was not found to be a misidentification following investigation.

GioHomeStudio reserves the right to terminate accounts with fewer than three strikes where the infringement is severe, systematic, or commercial in nature.

## 7. Abuse of This Process

Filing a false or bad-faith takedown notice may expose you to legal liability. Under 17 U.S.C. § 512(f) and applicable Nigerian law, any person who knowingly misrepresents that content is infringing, or that content was removed by mistake, may be liable for damages including costs and legal fees incurred by GioHomeStudio or the affected user.
