## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use a web application. They allow the application to remember information about your visit — for example, your login session or your preferences — so that your next visit is smoother and more personalised.

GioHomeStudio also uses similar technologies such as localStorage, sessionStorage, and server-side tokens that serve comparable functions to cookies.

---

## 2. Categories of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the platform to function. Without them, core services — including login, session management, and security controls — cannot operate. These cookies cannot be disabled.

| Cookie / Token | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Session token (server-side) | Authenticates your logged-in session | Session (deleted on logout) |
| CSRF token | Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks | Session |
| Auth state cookie | Maintains authentication state across page reloads | Session or up to 30 days (remember me) |

**Legal basis:** These are strictly necessary for the provision of a service explicitly requested by you. No consent is required.

### 2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the platform to remember your preferences and improve your experience. Disabling them may reduce functionality.

| Cookie / Storage Key | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Recent projects (localStorage) | Stores the list of your recently accessed projects for quick access | Until cleared by user |
| Sidebar state (localStorage) | Remembers whether the sidebar is collapsed or expanded | Until cleared by user |
| Theme / display preferences (localStorage) | Stores your selected display settings | Until cleared by user |
| Last active planner (localStorage) | Remembers which planner you were last using | Until cleared by user |

**Legal basis:** Legitimate interests in providing a functional and consistent user experience. You may clear localStorage via your browser settings at any time.

### 2.3 Analytics Cookies

GioHomeStudio currently uses **no third-party analytics cookies**. If and when analytics tools are integrated (for example, for understanding feature usage), this section will be updated prior to their activation and users will be notified.

Any future analytics integration will:

- Be disclosed in this policy before activation
- Offer an opt-out mechanism
- Use data only to improve the platform

## 3. How to Control Cookies

### 3.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. All major browsers allow you to:

- View cookies that have been set
- Delete individual or all cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all third-party cookies

Note: Blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging in and using core platform features.

### 3.2 Clearing localStorage

To clear functional data stored in localStorage:
- Go to your browser's Developer Tools → Application → Local Storage → select the GioHomeStudio origin → delete entries
- Or use your browser's "Clear browsing data" function and select "Cookies and other site data"

### 3.3 Do Not Track

Some browsers transmit a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. GioHomeStudio currently does not respond to DNT signals. If this changes, this policy will be updated.
