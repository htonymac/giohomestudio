## 1. Introduction

GioHomeStudio ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of the individuals who use its platform. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your data.

This Policy is drafted in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019, the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, and, where applicable, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users accessing the platform from the European Economic Area.

By accessing or using the platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account and Identity Data

When you register, we collect:

- Full name or display name
- Email address
- Profile picture (where provided or imported via Google/social sign-in)
- Date of birth (for age verification purposes)
- Account creation date and login history

### 2.2 Content Data

When you use the platform, we store:

- Uploaded media (images, videos, audio files)
- Written prompts, scripts, captions, and instructions you provide
- AI-generated outputs produced under your account
- Approval and rejection history for each content item
- Project metadata (title, series, mode, destination)

### 2.3 Billing and Payment Data

When Finance Phase 2 launches, we will collect:

- Payment method details (processed by our payment processor — we do not store card numbers)
- Transaction history and credit balance
- Subscription plan and renewal dates

### 2.4 Technical and Telemetry Data

We automatically collect:

- IP address and approximate geolocation
- Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
- Session duration and feature usage patterns
- Error logs and crash reports
- API call logs (without exposing prompt content to third parties except as described in Section 4)

### 2.5 Connected Platform Data

Where you connect social media accounts:

- OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens (encrypted at rest)
- Connected account identifiers (not passwords)
- Platform-specific posting logs

| Data Category | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---|---|---|
| Account data | Account creation, authentication, customer support | Contract performance |

| Billing data | Processing payments, preventing fraud | Contract performance; legal obligation |

## 4. Third-Party AI Providers and Data Flows

To deliver the platform's AI generation capabilities, certain data — primarily your prompts, instructions, and uploaded media — is transmitted to third-party AI providers. Each provider processes your data in accordance with its own privacy policy.

The following providers are currently integrated:

| Provider | Service | Data Transmitted | Training Opt-Out Available |
|---|---|---|---|
| Anthropic (Claude) | LLM, prompt enhancement, scripting | Text prompts, story input | Yes — Anthropic's privacy controls |
| FAL.ai | Image and video generation | Text prompts, reference images | See FAL.ai privacy policy |
| Kie.ai | Music and media generation | Text prompts, audio parameters | See Kie.ai terms |
| Runway | Video generation | Text prompts, reference images/video | See Runway privacy policy |
| ElevenLabs | Voice synthesis, narration | Text scripts, voice settings | Yes — ElevenLabs account settings |
| MuAPI | Music generation | Text prompts, genre parameters | See MuAPI privacy policy |
| Segmind | Image and video generation | Text prompts, reference images | See Segmind privacy policy |
| Suno | Music generation | Text prompts, style parameters | See Suno privacy policy |

We do not transmit your email address, full name, billing details, or account credentials to AI providers. We transmit only the minimum content data required to fulfil your generation request.

Where a provider offers a training data opt-out mechanism, we encourage you to exercise that right via the provider's own settings. GioHomeStudio does not make representations on behalf of third-party providers regarding training data practices.

## 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account data | Duration of account + 90 days after deletion request |
| User-uploaded media | Until you delete the file or close your account |
| Generated outputs | Until you delete the project or close your account |
| Approval and audit logs | 12 months minimum; longer if required for legal compliance |
| Technical/error logs | 90 days rolling |
| Billing records | 7 years (statutory requirement under Nigerian law) |
| Fraud/abuse investigation records | As long as reasonably necessary to resolve the matter |

Even where you delete content, GioHomeStudio may retain limited records where necessary to comply with law, investigate abuse, or defend legal claims. See Section 12 of the Terms of Use.

## 6. Your Rights

Under the NDPR, NDPA 2023, and applicable law, you have the following rights:

| Right | Description | How to Exercise |
|---|---|---|
| Access | Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you | Email legal@giohomestudio.com |
| Correction | Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data | Email legal@giohomestudio.com |
| Deletion | Request deletion of your personal data | Account settings → Delete Account, or email us |
| Export / Portability | Request a machine-readable export of your data | Email legal@giohomestudio.com |
| Objection | Object to processing based on legitimate interests | Email legal@giohomestudio.com |
| Withdrawal of Consent | Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is consent-based) | Disconnect accounts or close account |
| Complaint | Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission | ndpc.gov.ng |

We will respond to all verified data rights requests within 30 days. Complex or high-volume requests may require up to 90 days with notice to you.

## 8. Children and Minimum Age

The platform is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at legal@giohomestudio.com and we will delete the account promptly.

For monetisation features (credits, subscriptions, commercial publishing), you must be at least 18 years of age.

## 9. Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest
- Encrypted storage of OAuth tokens
- Access controls and role-based permissions
- Regular security reviews

No system is impenetrable. In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and the appropriate regulatory authority within the timeframe required by applicable law.
