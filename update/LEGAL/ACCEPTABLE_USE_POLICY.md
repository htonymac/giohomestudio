## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the standards of conduct required of all users of the GioHomeStudio platform. It forms part of the Terms of Use and is incorporated by reference. By using the platform, you agree to abide by this AUP in full.

GioHomeStudio is an AI-assisted content creation and publishing tool. The power of the platform to generate realistic media — including video, voice, and images of real people — creates a corresponding responsibility to use it ethically, lawfully, and without harm to others.

---

## 2. Absolute Prohibitions

The following conduct is strictly prohibited under all circumstances, with no exceptions. Violations will result in immediate account termination, content removal, and may result in referral to law enforcement.

### 2.1 Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

You must not use the platform to create, generate, store, transmit, or publish any content that sexually depicts, exploits, or endangers a minor in any form. This includes fictional, animated, AI-generated, or photorealistic representations. This prohibition is absolute and non-negotiable. GioHomeStudio will report any known or suspected CSAM to law enforcement authorities including the Nigeria Police Force, the National Agency for the Prohibition of Trafficking in Persons (NAPTIP), and applicable international authorities.

### 2.2 Non-Consensual Intimate or Sexual Content

You must not create, publish, or distribute intimate or sexual content depicting any real person without that person's explicit and documented consent. This includes realistic AI-generated depictions.

### 2.3 Deepfakes Without Consent

You must not create convincing synthetic media (deepfakes) depicting a real person without that person's explicit and documented consent. A deepfake includes any AI-generated or AI-manipulated video, audio, or image that realistically depicts a person saying, doing, or expressing something they did not actually say, do, or express.

## 3. Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following conduct is prohibited and will result in enforcement action as described in Section 7:

### 3.1 Targeted Harassment and Hate Speech

You must not use the platform to create content that:

- Targets an individual or identifiable group with harassment, threats, intimidation, or coordinated abuse
- Incites, glorifies, or promotes hatred, discrimination, or violence against any person or group based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability, or any other protected characteristic
- Is designed to psychologically harm, humiliate, or bully a specific real person

### 3.2 Fraud, Impersonation, and Fake-News Pipelines

You must not use the platform to:

- Impersonate any real person, business, government body, or public institution in a deceptive or misleading manner
- Create fake news, disinformation, or deliberately false factual claims designed to deceive audiences at scale
- Build automated pipelines for the mass production and distribution of false or misleading content
- Create fake testimonials, fake endorsements, or fake reviews attributed to real persons
- Simulate or replicate official communications from any government, regulator, or financial institution to deceive recipients

### 3.3 Pirated or Unlicensed Source Media

You must not upload to the platform any media — including images, video footage, audio recordings, music, logos, or text — that you do not own or have a valid licence to use in the intended manner. Using pirated software, stolen assets, or unlicensed media to generate commercial content is prohibited. See the Sound Licensing Policy for detailed rules on audio.

### 3.4 Voice Cloning Without Consent

You must not clone, replicate, or synthesize the voice of any real person — whether using the platform's voice features or any other method — without the explicit, verifiable written consent of the person whose voice is being used. This rule applies to:

- Private individuals
- Public figures and celebrities
- Deceased persons
- Employees, contractors, or colleagues

The consent of the voice owner is required in every case. "Educational" or "satirical" framing does not exempt you from this requirement where the intended audience could reasonably mistake the cloned voice for authentic speech by that person.

### 3.5 Brand Impersonation

You must not use the platform to create content that:

- Replicates or mimics the brand identity, logos, trademarks, or trade dress of another business without authorisation
- Creates a false impression that your content is produced by, affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any third-party brand
- Uses competitor brand names or assets in a manner that is misleading, defamatory, or confusingly similar

### 3.6 Political Content Without Disclosure

Political content — including content that promotes a candidate, party, political ideology, referendum position, or government policy — must be clearly and prominently disclosed as political content in accordance with applicable Nigerian electoral law and the rules of any platform on which it is published. You must not use GioHomeStudio to:

- Create undisclosed political advertising
- Produce synthetic or AI-generated political content without disclosure
- Suppress or manipulate information about electoral processes

### 3.7 Content Involving Real People Without Permission

You must not create content that:

- Uses any real person's name, likeness, image, or voice in a commercial context without their consent
- Makes false factual statements about a real person that could damage their reputation (defamation)
- Reveals private information about a real person without their consent
- Sexualises or degrades a real person without their explicit consent

## 4. High-Risk Use Cases Requiring Extra Care

The following use cases are not automatically prohibited but require heightened care and — in some cases — additional disclosures:

- **Satire and parody:** Content satirising public figures is permitted where it is clearly and unambiguously labelled as satire and cannot be mistaken for factual reporting.
- **Dramatisation of real events:** You must clearly label dramatised or fictionalised re-enactments of real events.
- **AI-generated news-style content:** Must be clearly labelled as AI-generated and must not be framed as original journalism.
- **Health, medical, or financial advice:** AI-generated content in these categories must carry a clear disclaimer that it is not professional advice.

## 5. User Responsibility for Compliance

As established in Section 2.1 of the GioHomeStudio Legal Framework, you are the publisher of record for all content you approve. You are solely responsible for ensuring that your use of the platform complies with:

- Nigerian law, including the Cybercrimes (Prohibition, Prevention, etc.) Act 2015 (as amended), the Nigerian Copyright Act 2022, the Child Rights Act, and applicable defamation law
- The laws of any jurisdiction to which your published content is directed
- The policies of destination platforms (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc.)
- The terms of third-party AI providers
- This Acceptable Use Policy and the GioHomeStudio Terms of Use

## 6. AI Disclosure Responsibility

Where a social platform, regulator, advertiser, partner, or applicable law requires AI-generated or AI-assisted content to be disclosed as such, you are responsible for making that disclosure unless GioHomeStudio expressly performs it automatically. You must not falsely represent AI-generated content as wholly human-created where disclosure is legally required.

## 7. Enforcement

### 7.1 Enforcement Ladder

GioHomeStudio reserves the right to take enforcement action against any account where violations of this AUP are detected. Enforcement action will generally follow this ladder:

| Stage | Action |
|---|---|
| First substantiated violation (minor) | Written warning; content removed |
| Second violation or first serious violation | Temporary suspension (7–30 days); content removed |
| Third violation or single severe violation | Permanent account termination; all content removed |
| Absolute prohibition violation (Section 2) | Immediate permanent termination; law enforcement referral |

### 7.2 GioHomeStudio Discretion

GioHomeStudio may escalate enforcement action at any stage, bypass the ladder entirely, and act without prior notice where the violation involves child safety, imminent harm, or serious legal risk. GioHomeStudio may also take emergency action pending investigation.

### 7.3 Reporting Violations

To report content that you believe violates this AUP, contact: legal@giohomestudio.com. Include the relevant content identifier, a description of the violation, and any supporting information.
