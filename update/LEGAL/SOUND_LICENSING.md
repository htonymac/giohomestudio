## 3. Synchronisation Rights

"Sync rights" refer to the right to synchronise (pair) a piece of music with a video. Sync licensing is separate from distribution rights and is required whenever music is matched to moving images for distribution.

| Audio Source | Sync Rights for Video |
|---|---|
| GHS stock library (Pixabay and CC0) | Covered by the applicable stock licence — permitted |
| User-uploaded audio | At the user's own risk — you must hold sync rights independently |
| AI-generated music (paid plan) | Generally included per provider terms — verify per-provider |
| AI-generated music (free plan) | May be restricted to personal, non-commercial video only |
| Third-party commercial music (streaming services, etc.) | Not covered — you must obtain separate sync and master licences |

You must not synchronise commercially licensed music (e.g., music available on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music) to video produced on this platform without obtaining the necessary sync licence and master licence from the relevant rights holders and their representatives.

## 4. Commercial vs. Personal Use

GioHomeStudio content creation can be used for both personal and commercial purposes. Your obligations differ depending on use:

| Use Case | User Obligation |
|---|---|
| Personal / non-commercial (private viewing only) | Standard platform terms apply; check free-tier AI music restrictions |
| Commercial (monetised YouTube, paid advertising, brand promotion) | Must verify commercial licence for every audio asset; paid AI music plans required where applicable |
| Public broadcasting or large-scale distribution | May require PRO (Performing Rights Organisation) clearance in addition to sync rights; seek legal advice |
| Film, TV, or commercial production licensing | Seek independent legal advice; GHS stock and AI music may not suffice |
