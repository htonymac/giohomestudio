# 2. Product Summary

GHM Auto Content Creator is an AI-assisted content system inside GioHomeStudio that helps users turn their daily media, business activity, outings, products, events, and approved personal content into ready-to-review social content.

The system is designed to:
- access user-approved media from phone galleries or uploads
- detect content opportunities from daily activity
- suggest content ideas automatically
- generate drafts for image, video, AI-enhanced image, image-to-video, commercial content, and narrated content
- use the user’s own approved voice for narration when enabled
- send drafts for user review before spending credits or publishing
- connect to supported channels for posting and scheduling later
- block nudity, sexual content, impersonation abuse, and risky likeness misuse

This is **not** a reckless auto-posting tool.
This is a **review-first content operating system**.

# 3. Product Vision

GHM should behave like a personal AI content assistant that says:

**“Hi boss, let’s make content with your today’s activities.”**

Instead of waiting for the user to manually build every post, the software should intelligently detect opportunities from new approved media and turn them into content suggestions.

### Example transformations
- market outing -> behind-the-scenes business reel
- new outfit photos -> fashion post or reel
- event clips -> recap montage
- product photos -> commercial flyer or ad video
- property inspection clips -> narrated promo
- daily life moments -> personal brand story post

The system should help users move from:

**media on phone -> idea -> draft -> narration -> review -> approval -> publish**

That is the defining experience.

# 7. Main User Journey

## Full Vision Flow
1. User signs into GHM
2. User grants selected media access
3. GHM detects new approved media
4. GHM identifies content opportunities
5. GHM sends a smart prompt:
   - “Hi boss, let’s make content with your today’s activities.”
6. User chooses a content direction
7. GHM prepares a draft
8. GHM shows:
   - media used
   - prompt used
   - suggested content type
   - narration script
   - preview draft
   - estimated credits
9. User reviews the draft
10. User edits if needed
11. User approves generation
12. Credits are deducted only after approval
13. Final output renders
14. User publishes, schedules, exports, or sends for review

# 22. Identity and Likeness Rules

## Self Content
Allowed when the user has opted in and the content is within policy.

## Business-Authorized Content
Allowed when the user confirms they have rights for staff, models, clients, products, or other approved media.

## Third-Party Unknown Content
Blocked by default for cloning, commercial AI use, or likeness transformation until rights are confirmed.

## Mandatory Rights Confirmation
Before using another person’s image, voice, or video, the user should confirm:
- I have the legal right to use this content
- I have permission for AI editing/generation
- I have permission for voice synthesis if applicable
- I have permission for commercial use if relevant
- I accept responsibility for rights clearance

# 30. Legal Draft — User Media and Voice Protection

## User Content and Rights
By uploading, recording, importing, or authorizing any image, video, voice sample, or other likeness-related content through GHM, you represent that you own the content or have obtained all necessary permissions, licenses, rights, and consents to use, edit, transform, synthesize, narrate, publish, export, distribute, and commercially exploit that content where applicable.

## Third-Party Voice and Image Use
You may not use GHM to clone, imitate, synthesize, transform, animate, modify, or commercially exploit another person’s voice, face, likeness, image, or video unless you have obtained their explicit prior authorization and all other rights required by law, platform rules, and any relevant commercial arrangements.

## Voice Cloning Consent
If you enable voice cloning or custom voice narration, you confirm that the voice belongs to you or that you have obtained explicit permission from the voice owner for cloning, synthesis, narration, and commercial use where applicable.

## Prohibited Impersonation
GHM strictly prohibits deceptive impersonation, false endorsement, unauthorized voice cloning, misleading synthetic media, and any attempt to make it appear that a real person approved, spoke, appeared in, or endorsed content when they did not.

## Approval and Credit Authorization
GHM may generate previews, drafts, scripts, narration samples, and review outputs before final rendering. No paid final generation, export, or publish action should occur until the user approves the action and authorizes the associated credit use.

## Commercial Responsibility
If the user uses GHM for commercial promotions, advertising, endorsements, or branded content, the user remains solely responsible for securing all necessary rights and ensuring compliance with applicable law, platform rules, privacy obligations, publicity/personality rights, disclosure obligations, and intellectual property restrictions.

## Safety Enforcement
GHM may scan media, prompts, scripts, voices, outputs, and publishing actions for nudity, sexual content, impersonation risk, rights abuse, fraud indicators, and other policy violations. GHM may block, suspend, remove, disable, or refuse content and accounts where misuse or legal risk is detected.

## Indemnity
The user agrees to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless GHM, its owners, operators, affiliates, staff, and service providers from claims, losses, damages, liabilities, penalties, costs, and expenses arising from content the user uploads, records, clones, generates, approves, publishes, exports, distributes, or authorizes through the service.

## No Guarantee of Legal Clearance
Use of GHM’s safety checks, rights prompts, or approval flows does not guarantee that content is lawful, cleared, or safe for use. The user remains solely responsible for verifying authorization and compliance.
